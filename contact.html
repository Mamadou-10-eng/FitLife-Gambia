Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do I need to book before visiting?

No booking required for a tour! Just walk in during opening hours. For classes, Premium and Elite members can book via WhatsApp.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have free parking space for members. Motorcycle parking is also available near the entrance.

Can I try before I commit?

Absolutely! We offer a free one-day trial pass. Just bring a valid ID and come ready to work out.

What should I bring?

Bring workout clothes, trainers, a towel, and a water bottle. Lockers are available for your belongings.
