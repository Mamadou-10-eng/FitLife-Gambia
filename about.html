About FitLife Gambia

Our story, mission, and the team behind your fitness journey

Our Story

FitLife Gambia was founded in 2019 by fitness enthusiats who say the need for mordern, inclusive gym in the kololi areas. What started as a small studio with basic equipments has grown into one of the most popular fitness centers in the region.

We believe that fitness should be accessableto everyone,regardlessof there Experienced level.Whether you are taking your first step in to fitness or you are a season athelate,fitlife has something for you.

Our facility is located on the senegambia highway, easily accessable to both locals and visitors staying in the tourism areas.we've become a comunity hub where people not just to workout,but to connect, motivate each other and grow together.o

Our Mission

To empower individuals in The Gambia to live a helthier,stronger and more confident life through fitness education, quality facilities and supportive community.
